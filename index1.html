Frequently Asked Questions
Simply browse the shop, add items to your cart, then go to Checkout. Fill in your name, phone and delivery location — your order will be sent directly to our WhatsApp (0792405060). We confirm within minutes!
Delivery is a flat fee of Ksh 50 per order anywhere within Serem and surrounding Vihiga areas. Orders above Ksh 500 may qualify for free delivery during promotions.
We accept Cash and M-PESA on delivery. You pay our delivery rider when your order arrives — no upfront payment is needed online.
Same-day delivery for orders placed before 2pm. Orders placed later are delivered the next morning. Delivery within Serem typically takes 1–4 hours.
Yes! All fresh produce is sourced daily from local Vihiga farmers and is harvested the same morning. We never sell stale or day-old produce. If you're not satisfied, we offer a full refund or replacement.
Mtumba refers to quality second-hand clothing, shoes and bags. Every Mtumba item is personally inspected, cleaned and graded (A = Excellent, B = Good, C = Fair) before listing. We only stock items that are wearable and presentable.
Yes. If an item is not as described or arrives damaged, contact us via WhatsApp within 24 hours of delivery. We will arrange a replacement or refund. Fresh produce issues must be reported same day.
We currently deliver within Serem, Vihiga Town, Luanda, Emuhaya, Hamisi and Sabatia. If you're outside these areas, WhatsApp us and we'll advise the best option.
Yes! We welcome local farmers, traders and artisans to list their products on SOKO HURU. Contact us via WhatsApp at 0792405060 to discuss partnership terms. We help you reach more customers.
Our prices are updated regularly based on KNBS market data, KAMIS reports and local Vihiga retail prices. Vihiga County rural prices are typically 10–15% lower than Nairobi. Prices may vary slightly by season.

Our Foundation

SOKO HURU — Free Market

SOKO HURU means "Free Market" in Swahili. Founded in Serem, Vihiga County, we believe everyone deserves access to fresh, quality goods without barriers or unfair pricing.

We connect local farmers, producers and traders directly with households, cutting out unnecessary middlemen and ensuring the freshest products at the fairest prices every day.

Powered by Boma Yangu — SOKO HURU is part of the Boma Yangu ecosystem, connecting Kenyan households to local goods and services.
Our Mission
"To make quality food, fashion and essential goods accessible to every household in Vihiga County through a fair, transparent, and convenient online marketplace."
